ABOUT

HACKWAW brings the greatest developers and designers to create mind blowing the fun-enabled environment. HACKWAW aims to federate the next generation of hackers for the Polish innovation community. The goal of the event is to rapidly prototype and build new projects, powering through 2-day hackathon to present a finished hack on last day.

WHAT'S IN IT FOR YOU?

  • programming, designing, brainstorming for whole weekend,
  • meeting more people like you from various tech fields,
  • meeting top notch tech mentors,
  • learning and experiencing amazing things,
  • a lot of free food and energy drinks,
  • a chance to win great prizes,
  • becoming a part of new rising community,
  • special zone for chillout and power naps,
  • you'll be attending second and legendary event of a series of HACKWAWs 

HACKWAW#1 and HACKWAW#2: WHAT'S THE DIFFERENCE?

#1  |   only software projects                    #2  |  hardware projects encouraged  
#1  |   24h non-stop                                    #2  |  24h 12(); sleep(); 12() saturday-sunday
#1  |   25.04.2012                                         #2  |   10-11.11.2012   
#1  |   teaming up starts at the event    #2  |   teaming up starts before the event via hackathon.io   
#1  |   Theme: "a product"                         #2  |   Theme: "Robots, wearable electronics and mobile apps"
