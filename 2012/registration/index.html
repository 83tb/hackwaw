We have 80 spots available only! Also, for everyone who register before 27th of October and decide to hack a hardware project, we prepare a free tools and parts to hack on.

HOW TO REGISTER FOR HACKWAW#2?

1. Choose the type of ticket you want to buy.
2. Proceed with the registration process: fill basic information about you and pay via Paypal.
3. When you got to the confirmation page, carefully read the information we give you about the next steps - submitting your hack.
4. Go to http://hackwaw.hackathon.io/ to register your hack and click "Join HACKWAW 2".
5. Register and fill your profile.
6. Add new team to HACKWAW 2 and describe your project. If you're going to do a hardware project - include information about tools and parts you need to make it happen and we'll provide them.

If you still have some troubles, just drop us a line: hackwaw@hackwaw.com.

AND THAT'S IT! HOPE TO SEE YOU ALL ON HACKWAW#2!
