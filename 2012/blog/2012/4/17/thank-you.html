THANK YOU!

Guys, we still don’t have words to describe how much last weekend was amazing, awesome, great, best and wonderful. We spent a LOT of time trying to do the best we can to make this event the most hackerish for you, but we didn’t even expect such wonderful weekend. It was LEGENDARY, didn’t it?

It was our first time, so we didn’t expect you to believe in us, but now we have to thanks to all hackers, designers, supporters, SPONSORS, mentors and press for their belief. All this never could happen without amazing people, who have put so many effort in making this event unforgettable for you. We want to say thank you one more time to Black Lion, Microsoft, iSource, HP, Beast fund, Allegro Group, Monster Energy, Business Link AIP. You guys made this event, THANK YOU!

Also, me & Kuba have some special thanks to do: Jarek, Agata, Bartek, Krzysiek, Marek and Paula. Thanks for making this whole thing MUCH easier for us. It was just great to know that we don’t have to worry about every single thing and that some things just happen without our attention. THANKS for your help during and before weekend!

One more thank to awesome mentors, who committed their time and shared their knowledge and experience with those who need that. It was pleasure and honor for us to have you and we’re super happy that you’re a part of HACKWAW community.

And last, but definitely not least, thanks for being with us hackers and designers. We can’t even imagine a better bunch of strangers, who became a real community in one day. We hope you’re so excited about whats happen next as we are. Stay tuned for even more great events coming this year :)

See you!
Ola & Kuba

April 17, 2012 /Ola Sitarska /
