Introducing disruptive model of prizing for HACKWAW

We are happy to announce that iSource, an authorized distributor of Apple products, has just become our partner and a sponsor.


iSource is mainly known as a dealer of Apple products but they also have a very strong mobile development team (IOS and Android). It's a group of people dedicated to create apps for clients as well as their iSource own app ideas. Company is also offering support in electronic publishing aspect of a digital market.


Together with iSource we developed a disruptive model of.. PRIZES for HACKWAW. This means you'll be able to:

A) win a new, shiny iPad. And yes, we mean a new iPad,
B) win an opportunity to co-operate with iSource.
There are several ways you can do that, especially they can assist you in selling your product. And when it comes to monetizing, iSource can be really helpful.

So where's the disruption? :)

A) and B) comes as a bundle. This means your team can get an iPad if your product wins and you want to co-operate with iSource after an event.

BUT

Number of teams receiving this prize on HACKWAW is UNLIMITED. It means that if there'll be 7 teams worth getting the main prize - iSource will deliver 7 new iPads.

And that's a pretty damn "HACKATHONIC" -  because you're not competing with others. You're competing with yourself.
