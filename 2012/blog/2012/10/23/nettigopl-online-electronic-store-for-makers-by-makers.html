Nettigo.pl - online electronic store for makers by makers

Latest trends of internet of things, 3D printing, and huge success of kickstarter's Pebble, Lockitron and Printrbot -- all this were an amazing inspiration for making the second edition of HACKWAW more hardware. Now it's even better, because we have a partner who make this event accessible and more fun for everyone. 

Ladies and gentlemen, Nettigo.pl is a strategic partner of HACKWAW 2! 

Nettigo.pl is an online store delivering electronics, specialised in Arduino. Witold Rugowski create a place where you can buy Arduino and components to build your own system. They also systematically develop knowledge base and expand Arduino community in Poland. Hopefully, one day someone create an interesting and innovative project thanks to Nettigo.

The philosophy of Nettigo is based on customer experience, that's why they're always trying to keep track of magazine, ship fast and pack items carefully. They also use things they sell on daily basis and it's the only way to make sure that products are actually good. Real online electronic store for makers by makers.

Nettigo will be a strategic partner of HACKWAW, that's why everyone who decide to create a hardware project can submit a hack and ask for everything you need to make it happen.
Just register for HACKWAW and go to our online hackathon platform to submit your needs. Please, do that before 27th October - only then we'll have enough time to order parts & tools for you!

Also, make sure to check out Nettigo.pl blog. It's a great place to learn more about Arduino platform! Like Nettigo.pl on Facebook to keep track on latest news in electronic world.
