Mentors

Łukasz Jakóbiec, WeMakeRobots.com

Lukasz is owner of wemakerobots.com - site focused on electronics, design and creation of mobile robots. In love with Python and Django. Discovered beauty of Atmel AVR microcontrollers in 2004 and that shaped his approach to electronics. In past embedded systems and electronics lecturer in PJWSTK and member of hardware R&D team in O2.pl

Zbigniew Sobiecki, Macoscope

Zbigniew is a veteran of the IT and Telecommunication industries. In 2004, Zbigniew became the first employee and CTO of a VoIP startup, in which he single-handedly designed the voice network, implemented his own billing and accompanying systems, and also hired and trained a small IT team. He co-founded the leading Polish (and supposedly the largest European) microblogging platform, Blip.pl, and sold it to Gadu-Gadu S.A. (a subsidiary of Naspers, a NASDAQ-traded company).

Witold Rugowski, Nettigo

Passionate about computers and electronics since being a little child. He began his work as being UNIX administrator, later he became PHP developer responsible for designing and managing computer networks. In 2006 he started to use Ruby on Rails and that became his main tool. At the beginning of 2009 he returned to his old area of interest: electronics. This was the moment when frustration raised - it was difficult to buy Arduino hardware in Poland. As a result he established his own store - Nettigo.pl.

Krzysztof Kowalczyk, HardGamma

Managing Partner at an early stage VC fund HardGamma Ventures and founder of GammaRebels startup accelerator (among Highly Commended in The Europas 2011). Mentor with StartupHighway, Seedcamp, Springboard, StartupBootcamp and StartupSauna, referrer with HackFwd.com and advisor to StartupWiseGuys. Highly Commended as 'Best VC Partner of the Year' in The Europas 2011 awards.

Daniel Owsiański, Macoscope

Daniel Owsiański is an independent IT consultant and author of webankieta.pl - the leading Polish online survey system. Since 2006 Daniel has become a tireless evangelist of Ruby On Rails framework and Ruby itself. He contributes to the Ruby community in Poland. Daniel is well known as one of the founding fathers of Bootstrap.pl, a community of Polish startups, where people can share both their technical and business experience. 

Krzysztof Mioduszewski, T-Mobile

ICT Specialist at T-Mobile Poland Innovation Unit, responsible for seeking out and researching new technologies and services which bring breakthrough value for customers. Telco Java programmer, web developer mainly focused on voice services and web applications. Impressed by HTML5 possibilities, believes that web apps will play key role in the future of mobile platforms.
