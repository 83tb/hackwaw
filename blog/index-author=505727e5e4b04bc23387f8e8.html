And the winners are...

HACKWAW #1 is over now. We announced winners yesterday at 3pm and then went straight to beds. You probably already read awesome stories about HACKWAW at Pej.cz or MamStartup, but we feel like we have to document eveything and say it officially :)

The winners of first ever HACKWAW are two amazing projects:

FOOD ON ROUTE

It’s super simple and useful mobile app created by Marcin Krzyżanowski (@krzyzanowskim) and Krzysztof Figaj (@kfigaj): Find food while on route with mobile application. Judges have praised simplicity, real need for app like that and good execution in 24 hours. Congrats guys!

POCKET CONSOLE

Use your iPhone as a console controller and play with friends anywhere you want! Any two devices you have (iPhone, iPad, iPod touch) can be used together with a big screen TV to maximilize your entertainment. Awesome game and platform for other developers created by the team of 3: Wojtek Siudziński (@suda), Paweł Ludwiczak (@ludwiczakpawel) and Karol Kozimor (@kkmzr).


But it’s not over! We also gave some awards for runner-ups of HACKWAW:

SO SHAPE - Award for the most polished app. Application for people actively practicing fitness. Find the nearest fitness studio in your current location. It offers personalized activities and exercises, evaluating trainers, classes and even much more!

ALLEBEAT - Special award from Allegro mentors. Figure out the best deals on Allegro, immediately.

RE-EVENT - Award for one man army. Find your friends on concert or festival with a simple and fast mobile app.

FASTBUILD - Award for the biggest amount of code lines per teammate. Goal of the project is to optimize the process of package building for linux distros. Also giving feedback of process results in nice and easy way on dashboard screen.

A.D.W.C - Award for the best A.D.W.C.;) - a proper compositor for wayland! Make a adwm or dwm like "window manager".

That’s it. But I’m sure you know that everyone who survive 24 hours, didn’t go home, didn’t sleep, actually is a winner. You’re guys just best. Keep rocking and hustling!

TEAMS

FASTBUILD

Goal of the project is to optimize the process of package building for linux distros. Also giving feedback of process results in nice and easy way on dashboard screen. The idea came during development of our product: OMNI - we have several build servers building each license type package.

 

SOSHAPE

An application for people actively practicing fitness. Find the nearest fitness studio in your current location. It offers personalized activities and exercises, evaluating trainers, classes and even much more!

 

GARDEN DWARVES

Garden dWARves are a brave garden gnomes fighting for freedom with evil, totalitarian evil gnomes. garden dWARves is a multi-player web game. The core mechanics borrows many ideas of all time classics like Scorhed Earth, Worms... with some ingridients of Angry Birds.

 

PROJECT_13

Project 13 is an online learning platform. It treats teachers as a asset that we 'sell'. People will be able to search for a teacher that specializes in topic that is interesting to student. Main way of teaching will happen online withing our platform.

 

CITY CONNECT

Find connection between two cities with buses, trains, etc.

 

SILENT ADMIRER

Let shy people choose secretly their love among friends. The chosen person will learn about that only if she chooses him back using our facebook app.


LOGSIFTER

Logs analyzer (apache, postfix, syslog etc) - web and Android clients. Multiple instances support.


FESTIVEE

Create mobile app for your event in practically no time.

Let your attenders throw out leaflets, they would do it anyway. Festivee will make creating a mobile leaflets for your Music Festival, Business Conference or event Hackaton (!!) a breeze.

You just need to select features that are appropriate for your event (news? schedule? and how will your attenders get to your event - maybe you would like a map? - everything is here) and fill it up with data. Clicky, clicky, and you're done.

 

DINNERS

 

CITYPULS

Citypul.se goal is to show you where exactly the party boom is located in your town. We agregate information from various web services and filter them leaving you with only the crunchiest info of all.

 

ADWC

Finally a proper compositor for wayland! Make a adwm or dwm like "window manager".

adwm: http://sourceforge.net/p/adwm/home/Home/ dwm: http://dwm.suckless.org/ wayland: http://wayland.freedesktop.org/

 

PMART and RE-EVENT

PMART / We make startup framework for ourself, learning integration with payments systems.

RE-EVENT / 1. Create event on Facebook; 2. Automatically share your location to event members; 3. Profit ?


FOOD ON ROUTE

Find food while on route with mobile application.


APPWARS

We're taking a classic war game model, mixing it up a little with a RPG card game and use apps ( and their powers ) installed on your mobile device to kick each other' arses.


POCKET CONSOLE

Use your iPhone as a console controller and play with friends anywhere you want! Any two devices you have (iPhone, iPad, iPod touch) can be used together with a big screen TV to maximilize your entertainment. Simple as that! ;)

Are you ready to CODE?

For those who have dared to forget, we're remaining: HACKWAW is TOMORROW! Hope you're excited as well as we are. We want to help you prepare, that's why we created the list of things to do before you come to BLAIP tomorrow.

1. If you have an idea what to do during this weekend, prepare your 60 seconds pitch in polish. Forget about business model, target group or other boring stuff. Simply say what you want to create and who you need to accomplish your MVP during weekends. Keep this stupidly simple. Make sure your grandma understand your pitch.
2. Take a rest: next 24 hours will be extremely busy, so it's really important to stay focus, stay up, survive :)
3. Remember to pack everything: your laptop and charger are the most important part, but if you're going to get some sleep during HACKWAW, it's good to get also a sleeping bag.
4. It's good to setup your server. You don't want to waste a valuable time during HACKWAW for stuff like this.
5. Don't forget your ticket! :) It will really speed up registration process :)
6. Get a proper attitude and bring smile to the event. We're waiting for you from 9:45 am on Saturday! :)

See you tomorrow hackers!

A) win a new, shiny iPad. And yes, we mean a new iPad,
B) win an opportunity to co-operate with iSource.

What are we going to do at HACKWAW?

A LOT of you was asking us about HACKWAW agenda. How it will go, what are we going to do, how long you will have to hustle etc etc. So there it is, agenda for most exciting weekend of April.

9:45 - Registration starts. After first coffee and breakfest you can now register at HACKWAW. Come to this awesome place called BLAIP. It’s super near to Politechnika subway station (basically ON it) and when you find super gold building, you’re in the right place. Don’t forget your ticket, laptop and charger(!). If you’re going have some sleep during night, sleeping bag can be useful.

10:45 - HACKWAW Kick-off! Super-quick intro speeches. We won’t make it boring.

11:00 - Pitching. The stage is yours. If you have ANY idea what you want to do during next 24 hours, we’ll give you a one minute (60 seconds!) to pitch your idea. Just say what you want to create and what people you need. KISS.

11:45 - Team forming. Find your team, talk to people, mix ideas, choose and setup your workplace! :)

12:30 - HACKWAW BEGINS! Now you can start hack! You don’t have time to lose, only 23.5 hours left!

14:30 - Sandwiches arrive. If you gotten to get hungry, good news! Sandwiches have just arrived. Bon apetit!

15:00 - Mentors are here. This means you may consult your project with experienced people who come to our event as advisors. The one-on-one advice mentors give to teams can be priceless and the best thing is you may put it to use immediately :)

19:00 - Traditional hackathon meal. Of course, pizza is here :)

20:00 - Time for mini competition. Allegro will definitely come up with something!

00:00 - Beware - quadcopter distraction :)

03:00 - Even more warm tasty extra pizza for night fighters!

09:00 - Breeaaakfest! Fresh bread, roasted sausages, some vegetables and cereals.

12:00 - THE END. This is the moment we turn off the electricity in the building, it’s done, no one works anymore :)

12:15 - Final pitches!

13:00 - Prizes and awards. 

If you have any ideas and suggestions about agenda, feel free to comment!
