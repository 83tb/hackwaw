HI HACKERS, SAVE THE DATE: 10 - 11th November

Yes, HACKWAW is back again with endless amount of fresh energy, ideas and creativity. We can't wait to see you again in amazing space of Business Link AIP on 10-11th November. Everyone agreed that first event was excellent, but this time we're going to raise the bar even higher.

What is different in the HACKWAW #2?

-only software projects
+hardware projects encouraged  
-24h non-stop
+24h 12();sleep();12() saturday-sunday
-teaming up starts at the event
+teaming up starts before the event via hackathon.io
-Theme: "a product"
+Theme: "Robots, wearable electronics and mobile apps"
+Hardware teams get hardware from the organizer

On the first HACKWAW you proved that you can build a software project - web or mobile in 24 hours. So now we have this crazy idea that it IS POSSIBLE to build a robot or a hardware project, all in 24 hours. Can you prove it again?:)  Of course, we know not everyone is as crazy as ourselves, that's why you can also make a software project at HACKWAW.  To encourage you to be crazy enough and start a hardware project we prepared a little treat: everyone who will have an idea for hardware project and register to the event fast enough to tell us about it, will get tools, hardware, arduino.. everything you need to make this happen completely for free! 

Registration will open really soon, let's stay in touch on facebook or twitter, so you won't miss the deadline!
