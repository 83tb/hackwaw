And the winners are...

HACKWAW #1 is over now. We announced winners yesterday at 3pm and then went straight to beds. You probably already read awesome stories about HACKWAW at Pej.cz or MamStartup, but we feel like we have to document eveything and say it officially :)

The winners of first ever HACKWAW are two amazing projects:

FOOD ON ROUTE

It’s super simple and useful mobile app created by Marcin Krzyżanowski (@krzyzanowskim) and Krzysztof Figaj (@kfigaj): Find food while on route with mobile application. Judges have praised simplicity, real need for app like that and good execution in 24 hours. Congrats guys!

POCKET CONSOLE

Use your iPhone as a console controller and play with friends anywhere you want! Any two devices you have (iPhone, iPad, iPod touch) can be used together with a big screen TV to maximilize your entertainment. Awesome game and platform for other developers created by the team of 3: Wojtek Siudziński (@suda), Paweł Ludwiczak (@ludwiczakpawel) and Karol Kozimor (@kkmzr).


But it’s not over! We also gave some awards for runner-ups of HACKWAW:

SO SHAPE - Award for the most polished app. Application for people actively practicing fitness. Find the nearest fitness studio in your current location. It offers personalized activities and exercises, evaluating trainers, classes and even much more!

ALLEBEAT - Special award from Allegro mentors. Figure out the best deals on Allegro, immediately.

RE-EVENT - Award for one man army. Find your friends on concert or festival with a simple and fast mobile app.

FASTBUILD - Award for the biggest amount of code lines per teammate. Goal of the project is to optimize the process of package building for linux distros. Also giving feedback of process results in nice and easy way on dashboard screen.

A.D.W.C - Award for the best A.D.W.C.;) - a proper compositor for wayland! Make a adwm or dwm like "window manager".

That’s it. But I’m sure you know that everyone who survive 24 hours, didn’t go home, didn’t sleep, actually is a winner. You’re guys just best. Keep rocking and hustling!

April 16, 2012 /Ola Sitarska /
