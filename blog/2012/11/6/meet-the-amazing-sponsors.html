Meet the amazing sponsors!

Meet brands who help us make it happen. We'll always be grateful to amazing companies and people who help shaping the developer culture and community in Poland! 

T-Mobile is a general sponsor of HACKWAW 2. T-Mobile believes in innovative services created by external teams. They offers them the possibility to use developed network od T-Mobile and access to a wide range of customers. Both teams that are just starting and those more developed can apply to the T-Mobile program. They realise that interesting ideas are born every day around us. T-Mobile want to support them by a number of initiatives and products like Open API and Open Innovation to cooperate with startups that need help with development and launch.
To learn more, visit http://innowacje.t-mobile.pl/

HardGAMMA Ventures is the leading early stage VC fund in Poland. HardGamma also run a startup accelerator program for technology entrepreneurs called GammaRebels. It focuses on accelerating and developing  startups through mentoring, advising and sharing both business & technical knowledge. GammaRebels is an intensive 3 month long program held in Warsaw. The second edition starts November 2012. They provide you what is the most important for startups – advice, mentoring, and connections that you wouldn’t find anywhere else.

Nettigo.pl is an online store delivering electronics, specialised in Arduino. Witold Rugowski create a place where you can buy Arduino and components to build your own system. They also systematically develop knowledge base and expand Arduino community in Poland. Hopefully, one day someone create an interesting and innovative project thanks to Nettigo. The philosophy of Nettigo is based on customer experience, that's why they're always trying to keep track of magazine, ship fast and pack items carefully. They also use things they sell on daily basis and it's the only way to make sure that products are actually good.

AIP is a business development accelerator supporting the most innovative startup companies. They base their model on a sustainable business development programme under which they provide a system founded on three elements: enterprise, knowledge, and community, and thus enable your company to expand to the international market.

Research In Motion (RIM), a global leader in wireless innovation, revolutionized the mobile industry with the introduction of the BlackBerry® solution in 1999. BlackBerry products and services are used by millions of customers around the world to stay connected to the people and content that matter most throughout their day. The BlackBerry product line includes the BlackBerry® PlayBook™ tablet, the award-winning BlackBerry smartphone, software for businesses and accessories.

We're extremely happy to have them on board. We also believe you're happy about this as well. 

Let's say BIG and loud THANK YOU.
